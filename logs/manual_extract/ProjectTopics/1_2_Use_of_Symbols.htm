Use of Symbols 

This manual uses the following icons to point out important information or safety-related issues:

 

The information icon indicates important information and issues to be noted for the reader.

 

The caution icon indicates very important information or a warning. Ignoring this advice can result in personal injury or damage to software or equipment.
